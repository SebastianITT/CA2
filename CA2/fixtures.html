Juventus v. Barcelona

Tuesday 22nd November Turin , Italy

The team are off on their journey to Turin in the hope of securing their spot in the last 16 of Europes greatest club competition. The reigning Italian champions are looking to boost their chances as they go into the game needing a good result to strengthen their hopes of qualifying. Barcelona need just a draw to qualify with a game to spare.

Valencia v. Barcelona

Sunday 26th November Valencia , Spain

Top of the table we lie as we go to the high flying Valencia who are sure to give us a real test. Valencia are sitting in 2nd in the La Liga table and are well clear of Real Madrid in 3rd. Valencia hope to shorten the gap between us and them whilst we hope to get the 3 points which will boost our lead to 7 points as we approach the halfway point in the season.
