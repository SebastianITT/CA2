Joan Gamper founded with many others our beloved club way back in 1899 when football wasn't even known about in the region. The club has gone above and beyond what we could have asked of them. The club has underwent a huge transformation from moving from the old Les corts stadium to the present Camp Nou with a capacity of 100,000 people which is incredible to think we had a couple thousand people only 75 years ago , 'Oh how times have changed'.

In over 100 years of existence. Our club has managed to win all these major honours:
